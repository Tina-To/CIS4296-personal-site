Projects

I've worked on some neat things.

I created an ATM in my CIS 3309 course. In this application, you can view your checking or savings balance, withdraw from your checking or savings, deposit to your checking or savings, or transfer from checking to savings and vice versa. This project allowed our class to work with stream readers and stream writers.

During the summer of 2019, I worked in a genomics lab as part of their technology team with Sudhir Kumar. Over the summer, I learned PHP and web API to work on the new publication system. The publication system's purpose was to make viewing and adding publications to their existing website simple. In the past publication system, the web design was messy and made it difficult for both students and researchers to use. In the final product, the team and I created a user-friendly system that is easy to navigate and created a simple way for researchers to drop new papers to the website.

I created an email system in my CIS 3342 course. It works very similarly to outlook. Users can send emails, flag emails, delete emails, view emails, and create an account. There was also an admin view that can ban and unban users.
