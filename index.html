Projects

I've worked on some neat things.

I created an ATM machine in my CIS 3309 course. In this application you have the ability to view your checking or savings balance, withdraw from your checking or savings, deposit to your checking or savings, or transfer from checking to savings and vice versa. This project allowed our class to work with streamreaders and streamwriters

During the summer of 2019 I worked in a genomics lab as part of their technology team with Sudhir Kumar. Over the summer I learned php and web api to be able to work on the new project I was assigned to. The new publication system purpose was to make viewing and adding publications to their existing website simple. During the time their web design was messy and made it difficult for both students and researchers to use. The final product the team and I were able to make was a user friendly system that is easy to navigate and created a simple way for researchers to drop new papers to the website.

I created a Email system in my CIS3342 course. It works very similar to outlook. In this application you have the ability to send emails, flag emails, delete emails, view emails, and create an account. There was also an admin view with additional things it can do such as banning and unbanning users.
